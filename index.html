THE ARTIST

SONGS ROOTED IN REAL EXPERIENCE

PRAYZVIBES is an independent singer-songwriter project creating organic indie-folk inspired by nature, personal journeys and human connection.

Every song transforms lived moments into honest stories that invite listeners to slow down, reflect and reconnect.

THE SIDE PROJECT

PRAYZ REMIX

A SECOND LIFE FOR THE SONGS

PRAYZ REMIX explores what happens when the emotional core of a song moves into a different world. Acoustic material is reshaped through electronic texture, pulse and colour while keeping the original human story intact.

The project creates a bridge between reflective songwriting and more rhythmic listening spaces.
