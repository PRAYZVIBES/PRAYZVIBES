Bavaria · built on the road

A cinematic space for dreamers & seekers.

PRAYZVIBES is an independent indie-folk project from Bavaria, blending intimate vocals, acoustic sounds and atmospheric production.

Inspired by nature, personal journeys and human connection, the music explores healing, freedom and meaning through honest storytelling.

Written, performed and produced by PRAYZVIBES. Shared in English, German and French—and wherever a song can travel without translation.
