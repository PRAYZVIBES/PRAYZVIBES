The road so far

Every place becomes part of the music.

PRAYZVIBES began on the street—sharing songs by Lake Chiemsee, in Lyon, on the island of Koh Phangan, and among the festival crowds of Rudolstadt.

Each place and unexpected encounter has become part of the music and the journey behind it.

Compact setup

The live rig is intentionally flexible: acoustic guitar, vocal microphone, compact mixer, amplifier and foot percussion. Optional textures can include didgeridoo, Ableton Push or Push Move, controller integration and ambient lighting.

Battery-powered performance for several hours is possible. No fixed stage size is required, though enough room to play and operate foot percussion comfortably is appreciated.
