Artist biography

Music that walks beside you.

PRAYZVIBES is an independent indie-folk project from Bavaria, blending intimate vocals, acoustic sounds and atmospheric production.

Inspired by nature, personal journeys and human connection, the music explores healing, freedom and meaning through honest storytelling. Written, performed and produced independently, the songs create a cinematic space for dreamers and seekers.

The project grew from a small home-recording space into live encounters on streets and festival grounds across Europe and beyond—from Lake Chiemsee and Lyon to Koh Phangan and Rudolstadt.

Live profile

Flexible, intimate and ready for the road.

PRAYZVIBES performs solo with vocals, acoustic guitar and foot percussion. Sets combine original songs with selected covers and usually run for 1–2 hours, with shorter or custom formats available by arrangement.

Suitable for festivals, intimate venues, private events and street-culture events. Travel considered upon request.
