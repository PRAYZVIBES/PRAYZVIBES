About
PRAYZVIBES

A faceless project with a deeply human centre—built from acoustic roots, honest words and the places music can carry us.

Independent by nature

A cinematic space for dreamers and seekers.

PRAYZVIBES is an independent indie-folk project from Bavaria, blending intimate vocals, acoustic sounds and atmospheric production.

Inspired by nature, personal journeys and human connection, the music explores healing, freedom and meaning through honest storytelling.

Every song is written, performed and produced independently by PRAYZVIBES. The aim is simple: make something true enough to travel with somebody.

Born between solitude and connection

From a wooden attic to the street.

Most songs begin with a feeling, a question or an experience that refuses to disappear. In a small home-recording space, those fragments become acoustic arrangements, layered atmospheres and lyrics that leave room for the listener’s own story.

Outside the studio, PRAYZVIBES grew through real encounters—busking by Lake Chiemsee, in Lyon, on the island of Koh Phangan and among the festival crowds of Rudolstadt.

“Music often reaches the heart before the mind.”

That idea sits at the centre of PRAYZVIBES. A song does not need to explain everything. It needs to create a space where people can slow down, feel honestly and find their own meaning.
