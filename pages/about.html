THE ARTIST

PRAYZVIBES is an independent singer-songwriter project from Bavaria, Germany, creating organic indie-folk rooted in storytelling, nature and human connection.


Blending intimate vocals, acoustic instruments and atmospheric production, PRAYZVIBES transforms personal experiences, journeys and moments of reflection into songs about healing, freedom and the shared human experience.


Inspired by the landscapes of the Alps and the quiet beauty of the Franconian Switzerland region, the music creates a space that feels both intimate and cinematic — inviting listeners to slow down, reconnect and find their own meaning within each song.


Built independently from songwriting and recording to production and visual identity, PRAYZVIBES represents a growing musical world where honest stories, emotional depth and natural sounds come together.


For dreamers, seekers and anyone searching for connection:
