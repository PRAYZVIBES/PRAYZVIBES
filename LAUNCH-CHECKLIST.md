# PRAYZVIBES launch checklist

Check each box after the GitHub upload.

## Website

- [ ] Homepage opens at `https://www.prayzvibes.com/`
- [ ] Mobile menu opens, closes and responds to Escape
- [ ] Mountain Day pre-save and Bandcamp buttons work
- [ ] Transience Bandcamp, CD/vinyl and store buttons work
- [ ] All four release covers open the correct song
- [ ] PRAYZ LIFE, PRAYZ STREETS and PRAYZ & CO open Spotify
- [ ] Live, About, Listen, Connect and EPK pages open
- [ ] Press articles open in new tabs
- [ ] Newsletter sends the confirmation email
- [ ] YouTube video loads only after clicking play

## Privacy and analytics

- [ ] Choose “Essential only” and confirm no Google Analytics request is made
- [ ] Open Cookie settings, enable Analytics and save
- [ ] Confirm Google Analytics receives a visit; it can take a little time to appear
- [ ] Test the Privacy and Imprint pages

## Search and sharing

- [ ] Open `/robots.txt`, `/sitemap.xml` and `/feed.xml`
- [ ] Submit `https://www.prayzvibes.com/sitemap.xml` in Google Search Console
- [ ] Share the homepage once in a private message and check the preview image

## Immediately after launch

- [ ] Create/connect the Meta Pixel used for advertising
- [ ] Update consent controls and Privacy Policy before activating it
- [ ] Test it in Meta Events Manager before the next campaign
